--Behind the Scenes:--

SCENE 1: INT. BEDROOM – 2018

The journey begins. A curious mind takes its first steps into the vast realm of Computer Science.

SCENE 2: INT. STUDY DESK – 2020

Arnav writes his first lines of Java code. The syntax may look strange, but the spark of passion is undeniable.

SCENE 3: EXT. INTER-SCHOOL COMPETITION HALL – 2021

The pressure is high. Arnav rises to the occasion, claiming the 3rd position and proving that this story is just getting started.

SCENE 4: INT. EXAMINATION ROOM – 2023

Months of preparation culminate in triumph — a perfect 100 in Computer Science board exams. The audience erupts in silent applause.

SCENE 5: MONTAGE – 2023 TO 2025

The world of JEE consumes time and energy. For two years, the keyboard stays quiet. But the story doesn’t pause — Arnav keeps watching, learning, following every spark of coding and tech news like an unseen narrator in the background.

SCENE 6: INT. SCALER SCHOOL OF TECHNOLOGY – 2025

The comeback. After a brief intermission, Arnav returns to coding — stronger, sharper, and ready for Act II of his saga.
